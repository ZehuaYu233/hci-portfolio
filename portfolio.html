My expertise is rooted in the intersection of Computer Science (B.Eng in AI Track) and Fashion Design.

I focus on creating embodied interactive prototypes and conducting user-centered research to empower sustainable practices. My work spans from building WebML/WebGL installations for somatic awareness to utilizing LLMs for qualitative meaning-making in garment repurposing.

I am driven to explore how Artificial Intelligence can act as a catalyst for sustainable HCI and facilitate deeper emotional connections between humans, intelligent systems, and materiality.
